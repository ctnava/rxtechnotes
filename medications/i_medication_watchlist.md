## üíö Digoxin (Lanoxin)

Digoxin is a **cardiac glycoside** used to treat heart failure and atrial fibrillation by increasing cardiac contractility and slowing heart rate.

- Tablets (0.125 mg, 0.25 mg)
- Oral solution
- IV formulation

**üìù Standard Sig:**

> Take 1 tablet by mouth once daily. Dose varies based on renal function and therapeutic drug monitoring.

- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Narrow Therapeutic Index**: monitor serum digoxin levels
- ü©∏ **Toxicity signs**: N/V, confusion, visual changes (yellow/green halos), arrhythmias
- ‚öñÔ∏è **Renally cleared**: Dose varies based on renal function and therapeutic drug monitoring.
- üßæ **Drug interactions**: Amiodarone, verapamil, and certain diuretics may increase toxicity risk

**üìù Standard Sig:**
